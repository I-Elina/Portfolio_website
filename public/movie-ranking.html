The Best Movies According to Shree

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Chennai Express

This is my favourite movie of SRK and Deepika Padukone. The scenes are soothing relaxing and at the same time have a bit of thriller in it.

Robot

Really cool sci-fi movie. It shows us robots if malfunctioned can be the biggest danger of mankind.

Laathi

Super beautiful film. Really artistic. Really focused on delivering essential values of life.
